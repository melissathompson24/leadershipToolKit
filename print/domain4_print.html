Domain 4: Leading in Context

This domain involves the ability to build collaboratives internally and with other federal agencies, state and local governments, tribal organizations, and nonprofit and private sector organizations to achieve common goals.

Competency A: Partnering

  • Develops networks and builds alliances.

  • Collaborates across boundaries to build strategic relationships and achieve common goals.
