Team Building/Team Unifying

Activity 2c.A1 (Domain 2)

Conduct team-building activities at a team meeting to facilitate communication and problem solving.

Determine the goals for the team-building activities. Seek out potential activities from a variety of resources, including the Internet, books, and colleagues. Conduct the activity(ies) and assess their effectiveness.

Websites with team-building activities include:

  • Team Building Exercises & Websites

  • Education Games from University of North Carolina

GOAL:

To apply competencies in fostering team cohesion and motivation.

  • Supervisor
