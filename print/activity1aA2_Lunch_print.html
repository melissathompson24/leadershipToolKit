Best Practice Approaches: Lunch & Learn

Activity 1a.A2 (Domain 1)

Review a professional journal such as the Journal of Public Child Welfare, Child Welfare, or Administration in Social Work.

Alternatively, if you have limited access to professional journals, check MyNCWWI's Resources for resource lists on a variety topics, including evidenced-based practice approaches related to those topics.

  • What best practice approaches that you have read about could you adapt for use at your agency?

  • What do you find particularly intriguing? Sponsor a lunchtime learning event to discuss the innovative approach.

  • How could you approach that task, process, or approach differently?

GOAL:

To explore new ideas and innovations to address agency issues.

RELEVANT FOR:

  • Middle Manager

  • Supervisor

  • Caseworker
