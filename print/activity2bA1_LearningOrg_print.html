Creating a Learning Organization

Activity 2b.A1 (Domain 2)

Look at your programmatic area and consider what you can do to promote a culture that encourages learning for all staff.

For example, do you...

  • Recognize and allow for different learning styles, including cultural differences?

  • Allow staff to bring mistakes to your attention and use them as learning opportunities (rather than as punishment)?

  • Mentor new staff in needed aspects of their work?

  • Serve as a mentor to incoming new staff?

  • Bring new resources and articles in for discussion and learning?

  • Support staff in training session attendance?

  • Ask staff to present what they learned from training to you and others in the unit or program area you supervise?

  • Offer a “book club” or “article swap” or otherwise encourage ongoing learning?
