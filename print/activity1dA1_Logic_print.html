Logic Modeling

Activity 1d.A1 (Domain 1)

Practice developing a logic model by applying it to a current or planned initiative at your agency (for example, a diligent search or adoption recruitment program).

Go to Worksheet 1d.A1, which includes an example as well as a blank worksheet. Complete it for your chosen initiative.
