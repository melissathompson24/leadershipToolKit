“Why People Stay” Interviews

Activity 2b.A2 (Domain 2)

Find out why people stay with the agency.

Interview multiple people to learn what keeps them at your agency. Such information will lead to a strengths-based approach to promoting a positive organizational climate.

Compile the information to inform strategies for maintaining a culture where staff want to stay.
