Dissecting a Decision

Activity 3d.A1 (Domain 3)

Think about a significant recent decision (made by yourself or by your agency) and dissect that decision.

  • What factors did you (or your agency) consider in that decision?

  • What quantitative or qualitative data informed the decision or was disregarded?

  • What alternative consequences could have resulted from the decision? Who was involved with the decision?

  • After the decision was made, did events unfold as anticipated?

  • If not, what did happen, and why do you think it happened the way it did?
