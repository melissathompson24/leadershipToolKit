Easing Compassion Fatigue

Activity 5d.A3 (Domain 5)

Sponsor a Lunch & Learn series on compassion fatigue to help staff cope with the challenges and stressors of their jobs. Determine specific topics and make arrangements for the events.

Just how stressed are you and your staff?

One way of finding out is to take the Professional Quality of Life assessment at ProQuol. This free assessment measures the negative and positive effects of helping others who experience suffering and trauma. The ProQoL has subscales for compassion satisfaction, burnout, and compassion fatigue.
