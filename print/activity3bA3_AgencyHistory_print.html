What’s Old is New: Agency History Project

Activity 3b.A3 (Domain 3)

Create a project to tell the story of the agency’s history. It could be in pictures, PowerPoint, or a document.

  • What does the next generation of leaders need to know about your agency?

  • In what ways does history impact and inform your agency (tribal or state)?

  • In what ways does history impact and inform work between tribal agencies and state and federal agencies?

GOAL:

To understand the history behind the agency and how it informs the future.

RELEVANT FOR:

  • Middle Manager

  • Supervisor
