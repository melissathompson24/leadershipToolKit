Legislative Review

Activity 1b.A2 (Domain 1)

Conduct a local legislative review to identify policies and trends that affect your agency.

Check out websites such as your state’s government website, the Children’s Bureau website, the Children’s Defense Fund, National Child Welfare League of America, and the National Conference on State Legislators.

Use the questions on Worksheet 1b.A2 as a guide to your review.
