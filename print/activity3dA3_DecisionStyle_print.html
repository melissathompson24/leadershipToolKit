What’s My Decision-Making Style?

Activity 3d.A3 (Domain 3)

Everyone, whether they know it or not, has an approach to making decisions.

Your approach is informed by cultural norms as well as your personality.

Assess your decision-making style by completing Worksheet 3d.A3. After assessing your style, respond to the reflective questions on Worksheet 3d.A3.
