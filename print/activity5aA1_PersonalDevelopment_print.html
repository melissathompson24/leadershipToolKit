Personal Professional Development

Activity 5a.A1 (Domain 5)

Examine your strengths and challenges and develop a professional plan for yourself.

Consider areas you are interested in but may not have a strong foundation in, and figure out ways to improve your knowledge and skills. Note that you can complete this activity in tandem with the Domain 3 (Competency: Planning and Organizing) Activity 3g.A2: Professional Development Plan.

Complete Worksheet 5a.A1. Be sure to share your plan with your manager/supervisor. Consider sharing the plan or parts of the plan with your team.
