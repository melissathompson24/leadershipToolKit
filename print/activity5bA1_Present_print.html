Present!

Activity 5b.A1 (Domain 5)

Once again, determine a topic you are passionate about and submit a proposal to present on this topic.

Watch for conference “Call for Papers” announcements in your area and nationally. If no such conference opportunity presents itself, consider presenting to your agency or to a community group to inform, educate, and inspire people about your topic.

Presentations to small, local groups also can offer a trial run when you are preparing a conference presentation for a broader audience.

GOAL:

To improve public speaking ability through a conference presentation.

RELEVANT FOR:

  • Executive

  • Middle Manager

  • Supervisor

  • Caseworker
