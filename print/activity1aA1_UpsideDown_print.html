Upside Down, Inside Out

Activity 1a.A1 (Domain 1)

An old adage says, “If you’ve done what you’ve always done, you’ll get what you’ve always gotten” (attributed to Henry Ford).

Think about a task, a process, or approach used by you and/or your agency.

What if you approached it from a completely different angle and looked at it from a fresh perspective.

How could you approach that task, process, or approach differently?
