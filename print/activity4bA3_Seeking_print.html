Seeking to Understand

Activity 4b.A3 (Domain 4)

Set up a face-to-face meeting with someone whose perspective typically differs from yours.

Treat this as a journalistic endeavor where you are seeking to understand the basis for his/her opinions and positions.

Use the questions on Worksheet 4b.A3 to guide the process.

Worksheet 4b.A3 - Seeking to Understand (PDF)

GOAL:

To gain a more thorough understanding of alternative viewpoints and the rationale behind them.

RELEVANT FOR:

  • Middle Manager

  • Supervisor

  • Caseworker
