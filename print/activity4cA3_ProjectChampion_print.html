Project Champion

Activity 4c.A3 (Domain 4)

Target a project or initiative you are passionate about and champion the project with staff and community.

How can you get others to believe in it as ardently as you do?

Remember, this is a personal learning experience to improve your leadership skills, not a full-scale, agency change initiative.

GOAL:

To provide an opportunity to persuade others about a personally important initiative or issue.

RELEVANT FOR:

  • Middle Manager

  • Supervisor

  • Caseworker
